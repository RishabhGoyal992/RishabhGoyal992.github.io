About Us

The Deapartment of Visual Media (DVM) of BITS Pilani is an all-student organization responsible for making and maintaining the Websites, Applications, Teasers, Trailers and Videos of the college fests. The Deapartment of Visual Media contributes in OASIS(The Cultural fest of BITS Pilani) and APOGEE(The Technical fest of BITS Pilani).

Achievements

The members of The Deapartment of Visual Media (DVM) of BITS Pilani have been known for setting up standards high by their work both off and on college. While there are a number of achievements and projects that have been carried out by the members of DVM, there are a few that are noteworthy and have won recognition and accolades from peers and industry.

Here are a few such achievements:

  • The members of DVM have done internships and also have been placed in IT giants such as Google, Facebook, Microsoft, Samsung and Cisco etc.
  • More than 80% of the GSoCers from BITS Pilani are DVM Members
